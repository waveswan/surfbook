## Steps For Judging Qualification

## Level 1 ISA Trainee Judge

Accreditation will be given to course participants
who meet the following criteria:
Be a member of your National Sporting
Association
Must complete the workbook and pass the
examination
You will be registered with ISA as an accredited
Level 1 official. This will enable you to officiate as a
trainee at events.
Payment of your registration fee will include you to
the International Officials database where you will
receive your electronic ID card and certificate and
be qualified on the ISA website with access to ISA
news and information.

## Level 2 ISA National Judge

Accreditation will be given to course participants
who meet the following criteria:
Be a member of your National Sporting
Association.
Must complete the workbook and pass the
examination.
Provide diary sheet detailing 20 hours of
practical judging experience.
The NSO Head Judge must sign off on these hours
on the provided diary sheet. You will be registered
as a National level 2 official with ISA and the
National Sporting Association. This will enable you
to officiate at sanctioned surfing events in a paid
capacity.
Payment of your registration fee will include you to
the International Officials database where you will
receive your electronic ID card and certificate and
be qualified on the ISA website with access to ISA
news and information.

## Level 3 International Judge

Accreditation will be given to course participants
who meet the following criteria:
Be a member of your National Sporting
Association.
Must complete the workbook and pass the
examination.
Provide diary sheet detailing 100 hours
of practical judging experience under a
qualified ISA

## Head Judge

The Head Judge must sign off on these hours on
the provided diary sheet.
You will be registered as an International level 3
official with the ISA. This will enable you to
officiate at regional, state, National and
International surfing events in a paid capacity and
be eligible for the international exchange programs.
Payment of your registration fee will add you to
the International Officials database where you will
receive your electronic ID card and certificate and
be qualified on the ISA website with access to ISA
news and information.

## Recognition Of Prior Learning (RPL)

If you have past judging experience you will not
be required to complete the practical hours
(Subject to completing and submitting the RPL
form to ISA)
Must be a financial member of the ISA
Judge’s Register

## Judging Re-Accreditations

Due in December each year visit www.isasurf.org
for more info.
