## The Official Code Of Ethics And Conduct

At no time will a judge give to anyone, any advice
or information pertaining to an event or to the
chances of a competitor or competitors, where
this information is confidential or outside the
scope of the judge at the time
At no time will a judge’s score be influenced by
personal relationships with any competitor or
competitors or by having a relationship with any
sponsor of an event, competitor or judge
A judge must at all times be punctual and meet
the attendance requirements assigned within
the judging roster as set by the Head Judge
on any day of an event. If a judge is not able to
meet their attendance commitment from reason,
every attempt must be given to notify the Head
Judge of intended absence in advance of that
commitment falling due
A Scoring Judge must be courteous and operate
respectfully under the direction of the Head
Judge, performing any judging/official duties as
directed by the Head Judge
No judge will attempt to purposefully distract,
by talking or other means, another judge from
performing their official duties whilst a heat is in
progress
Judges will refrain from openly copying other
judge’s scores or openly discussing scores,
unless such discussion is instigated by or
involves the Head Judge in cases of clear
uncertainty or missed rides

A judge may ask the Head Judge for an average
score for a ride
Judges will at all times wear the appropriate
official clothing, as given by the sponsors and/
or Contest Director and/or Head Judge, for the
purpose of event promotion
If a competitor requests information regarding
a heat that has recently been completed, they
should be referred to the Head Judge. Each
judge and official will value the individual in sport
Judges must wait until the completion of the tally
work before checking the completed tally sheets
No Judge of an event may pass comment on
a surfer’s chances in any event to the public,
media or other contestants. If this happens,
the judge in question will be dismissed from
the panel and other action will be taken by the
Board
Judges, once selected, must remain on the panel
for the duration of the event. If a judge forfeits
their position on the panel, he will be suspended
for a period to be determined by the Board and
may incur other penalties
All officials will place the safety and welfare of all
competitors and staff above all else and except
responsibility for all actions and decisions made
in the context of the competition
